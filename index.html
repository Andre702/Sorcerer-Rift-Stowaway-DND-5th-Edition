Sorcerer: Rift Stowaway

The concept of Magic is known to be everywhere and influence the flow of time and space. However not many are aware that there are places outside or even in between of what we call everywhere. Magic is a phenomena that connects all of those places with threads ever so thin they are imperceptible to even a trained eye. Sometimes however the threads of Arcana swell into veins or even rivers and as rivers in a heavy rain they tend to spill. In those rare moments and events that could occur in any place at any time the magic of places beyond influences the places of the known land and sometimes it influences also it’s people.

In consequence of some bizarre event that even the most knowledgeable struggle to describe your body, mind and soul has been imbued with the magic from beyond your realm. Maybe you or someone from your lineage has been exposed to the influence of the beyond during an extremely rare event that your people call the Dimensional Rift? Or perhaps one of your ancestors or even you yourself are a creature from beyond this realm who was caught by an opening rift and forever dragged away from your home only to end up here? Whatever was the path of fate that lead to this moment, you are now in possession of powers that you can only describe as distant. You can sense they do not come from you or any place around you as even when using them you can feel yourself becoming not the wielder but more like a conduit for this strange, alien Arcana.

Source: Antonio's Storries of Beyond

Rift Stowaway Quirks

At your option, you can pick any number from or roll any number of times on the Rift Stowaway Quirks table to create an interesting quirk or set of quirks for your character.

d6 Quirk
1 When you cast your spells they never originate from the tip of your fingers, but rather from a place right in front of them.
2 Your every spell leaves a vague trail of shattered fragments of space that look similar to shattered glass.
3 The formulas and gestures for the spells you cast seem to be different than the ‘proper’ ones used by other magic users.
4 Casting any spell causes a loud, almost deafening crack like noise heard only by you
5 When casting spells your veins sporadically light up white (or other color of your choosing)
6 Your voice changes drastically when you cast spells

Spells from the Rift

From 1st level you have the ability to channel your magic through a point in space where you will open a small rift in order to cast a spell from that point of origin.

When casting a spell that does not require concentration and is not described as a touch spell you can choose to cast if from a point 5 feet away from you. You cast a spell as if you were standing in a position which you chose.

When you reach 3rd level in this class, you gain 1 usage per long rest of the modified rope trick spell that opens an entrance to extradimensional space called the place between space. The differences being that the spell has a 5ft range and does not require rope as component. A creature can enter the extradimensional space by expending 5ft of movement. When the spell ends only creatures and every item in their possession are dropped out of the extradimensional space, other items remain inside the place between space. Additionally after casting the modified rope trick you need to maintain concentration for the whole duration of the spell. When you loose concentration on the spell or whenever the entrance closes the spell ends.

Space Shift

From 6th level you gain more control in the flow of space. By reading the constant flow of the threads of arcana you can quickly and swiftly tear a small rift right in front of you by using your hand.

By using your reaction and expending at least 1 sorcery point you can reduce the dame of a ranged attack projectile or throwing weapon in range of your Spells from the Rift feature by Nd4 (N being the number of expended sorcery points) If you reduce the damage of an attack to 0 the projectile or throwing weapon used in the attack gets transported into the place between space.

Adjustment to Beyond

At 14th level your Spells from the Rift feature’s range is extended to 10 feet and you can cast touch spells through small rifts you open.

Rift Mastery

At 18th level your Space Shift feature damage reduction is extended to Nd6 (N being the number of expended sorcery points). Additionally you can now use this feature against melee attacks and single target spells targeting you specifically or any other creature within your Spells from the Rift feature.
Weapons used in melee attacks reduced to 0 damage are not transported to the place between space. However if you reduce the damage of a single target spell to 0 this spell ends and you can recover a spell slot of level equal to the one you have transported to the place between space.
